Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Coding Club and how to get involved

Joining is easy! Fill out our application form online. We welcome students from all backgrounds and skill levels. Once you submit the form, we'll get in touch with you about upcoming meetings and events.

Not at all! We welcome members of all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced programmers. Our community is built on learning together and supporting each other's growth. We offer workshops and mentoring to help you get started.

We work with a variety of languages and technologies depending on project needs. Python is our most commonly used language for data science and AI projects, but we also work with JavaScript for web development, and other languages as needed. Our focus is on practical skills that can be applied to real-world problems.

We typically hold general meetings once every two weeks, with additional project-specific meetings as needed. We also organize workshops, hackathons, and special events throughout the academic year. Meeting schedules are announced through our communication channels.
